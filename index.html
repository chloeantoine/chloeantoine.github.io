About

Welcome! I am a 1st-year PhD student in economics at CREST — École Polytechnique, advised by Jean-Marc Bourgeon and Gregory Corcos.

My research interests lie in the fields of environmental economics and international trade, particularly when they apply to agriculture and biodiversity. Currently, my work aims at studying the consequences of agricultural specialisation, with both theoretical and empirical perspectives. I notably enjoy working with geospatial data.

I graduated from Institut Polytechnique de Paris in 2023, where I followed a master's degree in economics. Before that, I studied economics and history at Université Paris Sciences & Lettres as part of a multidisciplinary undergraduate program.

You can find more details in my CV.
